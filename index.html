FAQ

Common questions.

Short answers about sourcing from Taiwan, China, and wider Asia with La Varieta.

What does a sourcing agent in Taiwan do?

A sourcing agent finds and manages manufacturers on your behalf. La Varieta identifies suitable factories in Taiwan and China, collects quotes, coordinates samples, and follows production through quality control and shipping.

Which products can La Varieta source?

Almost anything manufactured in Taiwan or China. Pierre's deepest factory relationships are in fans, hygiene products, and LED — but over 40 years he has sourced everything from warehouse racking to construction materials. Send your product details and he will tell you honestly whether it is a fit.

I already found suppliers on Alibaba. Can you still help?

Yes. Many buyers come to us with a shortlist. Pierre reviews the suppliers, quotes, and samples, and helps you judge which factory is actually right — before money is committed.

How much does the Sourcing Review cost?

The initial review is a paid service for serious buyers. Contact us with your product details and we will send a clear quote before any commitment.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQ depends on the product and the factory. Tell us your target quantity in your brief — Pierre will tell you honestly whether it is realistic.

Do you handle shipping and logistics?

Yes. La Varieta coordinates quality control and the shipping handoff — export documents, freight coordination, and delivery follow-up — so your order does not stall at the port.
